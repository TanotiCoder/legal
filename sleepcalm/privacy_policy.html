Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sleep Calm (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our App. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use Sleep Calm.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy by design. Sleep Calm is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.

  • No Personal Account Required: You do not need to create an account, provide your name, or email address to use the App.
  • Local Storage: Your app preferences, custom mixes, sleep timers, and usage data are stored locally on your device using local databases (such as Hive and SharedPreferences). We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • Device Information: We may automatically collect limited device information (such as operating system version and device type) for troubleshooting and app improvement purposes.
  • Purchase Information: If you choose to upgrade to a premium subscription, your purchase is processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your credit card information. We receive generic purchase status information (via our in-app purchase systems) to grant you access to premium content.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (e.g., saving your favorite mixes).
  • Process your premium subscription status and unlock premium content.
  • Improve the overall user experience and performance of the App.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or process payments. These include:

  • Apple App Store & Google Play Store: For processing payments and managing subscriptions.

Please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Since your usage data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data relies on your device's security measures (e.g., passcode, biometric locks). We do not transmit your personal usage data across the internet to our servers. We use industry-standard practices for any temporary diagnostic data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data under regulations like the GDPR or CCPA. Because Sleep Calm stores data locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete all your data by simply uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data in your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@sleepcalm.app
